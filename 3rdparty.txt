The 3rdparty folder contains dependencies for 64-bit Windows.

OpenAL Soft 1.17.2 - http://kcat.strangesoft.net/openal.html
libpng 1.6.24 - http://www.libpng.org/pub/png/libpng.html
sqlite3 3.13.0 - https://www.sqlite.org/download.html
libogg 1.3.2, libvorbis 1.3.5 - https://www.xiph.org/downloads/
glm 0.9.7.6 - https://github.com/g-truc/glm/releases
zlib 1.2.8 - http://www.zlib.net/
dirent - https://github.com/tronkko/dirent
freetype 2.6.5 - https://www.freetype.org

All libraries were compiled using MSVC2015.
